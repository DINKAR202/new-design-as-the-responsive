Welcome To AJ Institute of Medical Sciences (AJIMS)

AJ Institute of Medical Sciences is the premier Medical institute established by educationist & philanthropist Mr. A J Shetty in 2002. The college aims to provide a global perspective of medical education and Health service to achieve the national goal of “Health for all”. The college offers UG, PG, and Diploma degrees in different specializations. The College has the best state-of-the-art infrastructure including well-equipped and modern classrooms, Laboratories with modern equipment, A central library, and many more which provide top-class medical education to the students.

    Accreditations & Approvals:

  • Approved by the Government of Karnataka
  • Affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences
  • Approved by National Medical Commission (NMC)
  • Approved by the MCI
  • Certified by NABH

    Reasons to study at AJIMS:

  • Campus Infrastructure: The campus is well-equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. It has modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a well-stocked library, and advanced medical equipment.
  • Highly Qualified Staff: The College boasts highly talented and experienced faculty members who are pledged to molding its students into highly disciplined intellectuals of integrity and fostering medical ethics and values.
  • Centre for Research (CoE): The college encourages research and innovation among its students. It provides them with opportunities to participate in research projects, present their work at conferences, and publish in scientific journals.
  • Integrated & Experiential Learning: The College aims to provide an opportunity to work alongside medical professionals to promote practical learning, and to expand their horizons and insights in the learning process.
  • Extracurricular Activities: The college organizes extracurricular activities very frequently, It helps in the all-around development of the students. The college encourages its students to participate in these activities.

Eligibility Criteria:

Under-Graduate

MBBS/Allied Health

● Students must be 17 years old by December 31st of the admission year ● Students must secure a minimum of 50% in PCB. In the case of SC/ST students, 40 % and 45% for OBC. ● For MBBS, Appearing in the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET-UG) is mandatory.

Post-Graduate

MD/MS

● Candidates seeking admission must have earned an MBBS degree from a recognized university. ● For MD/MS, Appearing in the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET-PG) is mandatory. ● Students must have completed a 1-year internship.

Allied Health

● Candidates must have passed and secured a Bachelor's degree in Allied Health Science Subjects from a recognized University.

Campus Life:

Accommodation Facility: AJIMS has separate hostels for boys and girls, It provides state-of-the-art accommodation facilities including spacious and well-furnished rooms, Laundry, a separate reading room, 24 x 7 wifi, and security surveillance.

Central Library:The college has one of the largest libraries, with a seating capacity of more than 500 students. It has well-equipped with modern facilities including e-books and e-journals.

Sports: The college takes care of the physical and mental fitness of its students. It provides world-class sports facilities including Sports grounds for cricket and football, a basketball and volleyball court, table tennis, a gym, and many more.

Auditorium: The college has a big Air-conditioned and well-equipped auditorium with a seating capacity of more than 500 people. This auditorium is also used for many cultural activities.

Student Organizations and Clubs: The college emphasizes the formation of the Students organization and clubs on the college campus for helping students in academic societies, research clubs, sports teams, cultural groups, and social services.

Cafeteria: A cafeteria is placed inside the college campus, It serves hygienic varieties of foods including North and South Indian dishes at nominal prices.
